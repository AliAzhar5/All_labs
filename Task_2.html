Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced ham
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper

Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Add milk, cheese, ham, and bell pepper to the bowl and mix.
  3. Pour mixture into a greased skillet and cook over medium heat.
  4. Stir occasionally until eggs are fully cooked.

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add chicken breasts to the bowl and coat with the marinade.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.

Stuffed Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce, then add pepperoni and olives.
  5. Roll up the dough and pinch the edges to seal.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
